Sprout writes a brand-new bedtime story starring your own child, every single night.

Tell Sprout your child's name, what they did today, and a gentle lesson — bravery, sharing, kindness — and Sprout writes a soft, soothing ~250-400 word story with your child as the kind, capable hero, plus a gentle illustrated scene. Tap play and Sprout reads it aloud on-device, highlighting each sentence as it goes.

Every story is saved to your Library so you can replay any favorite, any night, even offline.
